<h1>CPR Rhythm Simulation</h1>

#This game simulation lets you play a simplified simulation on how to administer CPR.
#The game is also full of interesting and useful information on the real life utility of CPR such as how to administer it and what positive effects it has on your family and community.
#The main goal of this game is to raise awareness of CPR and Basic Life Support. We aim to encourage people to go out and formally learn these topics.

Controls
Press [Spacebar] whenever the red bars intersect with the blue circle.
